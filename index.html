About Us

Home / About Us

CHANAKYA FOUNDATION Group of Institutions

was established in 2008 under Indian Trust Act, 1882. Since its inception in year 2008, we have been implementing various programs related to Skill Training, education, health care, economics, environment and empowerment of the downtrodden people, through State/ Central Govt. Welfare schemes in various States across India.

Our Vision is to be an institute of academic excellence in the field of education, With the future plan of becoming a deemed university, earn name and hence win faith of the society.

Our Mission is to impart our students highest quality education, develop their Skills, broaden their mental horizon, and nurture them into Competent and talented professionals to meet the challenges of the new millennium.

Our approach to imparting quality education is best conveyed by this quote from our esteemed Chairman Dr. Ashok Gagan: "Education is for improving the lives of others and for leaving your community better than you found it"

According to our CEO Dr. Divya Jyoti: "Education at Chanakya is not just about academic brilliance, but an ambience where culture and human skills blend together to make a better indivudual."

Chankya Foundation's Director Dr. Arjun Sinha believes highest quality education is every individuals birthright and as foundation's director he goes above and beyond stipulated guidelines of university by providing our students world class infrastucture, modern equipment and guest lectures from renowned faculties to augment studies beyond syllabus requirements.

We are proud to provide our students world class college experiece by providing state of art labrotaries, gyms, hostels and other amenities in our 200+ acre Campus. Chanakya foundation has tie ups with 50+ private and public companies for placement of our students.
